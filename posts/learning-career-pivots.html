Learning Career Pivots

How project shifts taught me adaptability.

Sometimes, projects take unexpected turns. A sudden client change, a vendor delay, or an internal restructure—it can throw the best-laid plans off balance.

I remember a project where half the resources shifted mid-way. Panic was the first reaction, but soon I realized this was an opportunity to learn. We re-prioritized, adjusted deliverables, and I learned how flexibility can be more valuable than rigid planning.

Pivoting is not about giving up control; it’s about seeing options you didn’t notice before.

True growth comes when you learn to dance with change rather than fight it.
