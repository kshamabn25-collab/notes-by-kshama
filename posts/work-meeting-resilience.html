The Meeting That Looked Perfect on Slides

A quiet lesson in reading what dashboards don’t say.

The deck was flawless. Green indicators everywhere. Risks logged, dependencies mapped, timelines aligned.

Yet the room felt heavy. Cameras stayed off. Answers were careful, not confident.

That meeting taught me something no framework did — projects speak through people, not charts.

Silence is sometimes the loudest risk indicator.
