I help complex programmes move from intention to execution — with clarity, rhythm, and disciplined follow-through.

My work sits at the intersection of delivery structure and people alignment: translating ambitious goals into practical plans, making risks visible early, and creating a reliable operating cadence so teams can deliver without chaos.

I’ve worked in large, multi-stakeholder environments where progress depends on dependency management, governance routines, and fast decision-making — not just tools and templates. I enjoy the “invisible” parts of delivery: stakeholder expectations, clean reporting, crisp follow-ups, and making sure the right conversations happen at the right time.

Outside formal programme work, I’ve built my voice through Toastmasters and through years of teaching and community leadership. Those experiences shaped how I facilitate, how I handle high-pressure discussions, and how I communicate with calm confidence — especially when situations are ambiguous.

This website is my living portfolio. Instead of compressing experience into bullet points, I’m building a public archive of how I think in practice — case studies, project artifacts, and writing that captures the lessons behind the outcomes.

What I’m optimising for

Predictable delivery, fewer surprises, and decisions that stay aligned to business outcomes. I care about building systems that make execution easier — not heavier.

How I work

Clear milestones, honest status, tight follow-ups, and simple dashboards. I prefer calm execution over “hero mode” — and I make dependencies and trade-offs explicit early.

What this space contains

Case studies and project artifacts you can actually read (not hidden in slide decks), plus writing on work, voice, and life — the parts that shape leadership over time.
