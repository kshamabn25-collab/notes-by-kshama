How to read these case study packs

This case study pack is created for educational and portfolio purposes. It is based on publicly available information and does not contain confidential or proprietary material.

What is real

  • The scenario theme and high-level context are grounded in publicly reported events and industry practice
  • The project constraints and risks reflect common realities in financial services transformation

What is reconstructed

  • The artifacts (charter, RAID, readiness checklist, status report) are reconstructed examples
  • Names, internal structures, and specific operational details are intentionally generalized
  • Any metrics shown are illustrative unless explicitly stated as public figures

How to use this pack

  • As a reference for structuring programme governance and delivery artifacts
  • As an interview discussion piece to explain how you think and operate under risk
  • As a learning guide for evidence-based readiness and operational resilience
