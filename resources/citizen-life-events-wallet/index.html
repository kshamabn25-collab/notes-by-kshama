Delivery case study pack — readable project artifacts (no downloads).

What this pack contains

This is a structured set of PM artifacts for a citizen-centric “life events” digital wallet concept. The aim is to show how I organise scope, governance, risk, and cost/resourcing in a way that’s clear enough to run.

Work Public Services Digital Transformation Hybrid Delivery PM Artifacts

At a glance

  • Programme: Irish Government Life Events Programme (Digital Wallet + MyGovID integration)
  • Timeline: Jan 2024 – Dec 2025
  • Budget: €25M
  • Core journeys: birth, death, marriage, driving, home ownership
  • Delivery approach: Hybrid governance (stage gates + compliance) with Agile Scrum delivery teams
  • Selected outcomes: 524K downloads (3 months), 99.7% uptime, 47% reduction in time-to-complete
