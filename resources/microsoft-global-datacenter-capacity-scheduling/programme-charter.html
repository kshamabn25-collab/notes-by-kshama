Programme Charter

Document control

  • Document ID: IMS-CHTR-001
  • Version: v1.0
  • Status: Baseline (Simulation)
  • Owner: Scheduling Program Manager

Purpose

Establish an Integrated Master Schedule (IMS) and schedule governance approach to deliver committed data center capacity across multiple regions. Ensure schedule integrity, dependency clarity, and risk-based mitigation to protect delivery dates.

Scope

  • Portfolio-level schedule integration across 5 regional programs
  • End-to-end milestones: civil → power → network → commissioning → operational handover
  • Critical path and near-critical monitoring using CPM methodology
  • Executive reporting, escalation thresholds, and decision cadence

Out of scope

  • Detailed engineering design content (captured in domain systems)
  • Vendor contract management (tracked by commercial owners)
  • Financial forecasting (handled via finance governance)

Success measures

  • Committed capacity dates maintained within agreed tolerance
  • Critical dependencies visible and actively managed
  • Schedule risks identified early via sensitivity scenarios
  • Leadership decisions supported with clear “impact-to-date” narratives

Governance

  • Weekly: Schedule Health Review (IMS + critical tasks + risks)
  • Bi-weekly: Dependency Resolution Forum (cross-segment closure)
  • Monthly: Executive Portfolio Review (forecast + decisions)
