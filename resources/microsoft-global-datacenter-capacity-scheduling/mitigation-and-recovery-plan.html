Mitigation & Recovery Plan

Mitigation actions (pre-emptive)

  • Parallelize approvals where feasible (documents + reviews)
  • Pull-ahead commissioning pre-work (test plans, staging, checklists)
  • Introduce buffers on high-uncertainty dependencies
  • Lock decision gates early for long-lead procurement

Recovery actions (when slippage is forecast)

  • Fast-track non-blocking tasks (overlap where risk is acceptable)
  • Resource shift between regions (targeted surge support)
  • Resequence activities to protect the committed milestone chain
  • Escalate utility and vendor bottlenecks with decision options

Decision gates

  • Gate A: forecast slip > 10 working days → recovery plan required
  • Gate B: committed date risk → exec decision on tradeoffs
