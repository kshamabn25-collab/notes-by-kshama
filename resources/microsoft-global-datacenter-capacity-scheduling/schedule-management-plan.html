Schedule Management Plan

Scheduling standards

  • All portfolio milestones must be traceable to a WBS element and owner
  • Dependencies must be explicit (FS/SS/FF relationships where applicable)
  • Critical milestones must have acceptance criteria and “definition of done”
  • Dates are forecasted based on evidence (site readiness, approvals, procurement signals)

CPM methodology

  • Critical path updated weekly
  • Near-critical band tracked (≤ 10 working days total float)
  • Float erosion flagged as early warning even if dates remain unchanged

Baselines & change control

  • Baseline set post-planning gate; changes require impact narrative
  • Any milestone movement affecting committed dates triggers Change Impact Analysis
  • Re-baseline only when scope changes or assumptions materially shift

Cadence

  • Weekly: IMS updates + critical path refresh + risk checks
  • Bi-weekly: dependency closure review
  • Monthly: executive dashboard + forecast confidence review

Escalation thresholds

  • Committed date risk > 5% probability or > 10 working days slip forecast
  • Total float drops below 5 working days on any critical milestone
  • Dependency breaches: downstream start blocked with no approved recovery
