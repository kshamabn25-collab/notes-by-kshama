Schedule Risk & Sensitivity Analysis

Primary risk drivers

  • Utility approval and energisation lead times
  • Long-lead procurement variability (global supply constraints)
  • Commissioning labour capacity and sequencing across sites
  • Interdependent handover windows (Ops readiness + compliance)

Sensitivity scenarios (modeled)

  • S1: +15 working days delay in power approval
  • S2: +30 working days delay in long-lead equipment delivery
  • S3: constrained commissioning teams (reduced throughput)

What triggers escalation

  • Forecast slip of committed milestone beyond tolerance
  • Critical milestone float drops below 5 working days
  • Repeated churn in forecast dates without root-cause closure
